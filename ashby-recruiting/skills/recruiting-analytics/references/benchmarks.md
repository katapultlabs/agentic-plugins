# Recruiting Benchmarks

Industry benchmarks for context when analyzing Katapult's recruiting metrics. These are general tech/software industry benchmarks — adjust based on role seniority, market conditions, and Katapult's historical data.

## Time-to-Fill Benchmarks (days)

| Role Type | Good | Average | Needs Attention |
|-----------|------|---------|-----------------|
| Junior/Entry Level | < 25 | 25-40 | > 40 |
| Mid-Level Engineer | < 35 | 35-50 | > 50 |
| Senior Engineer | < 45 | 45-65 | > 65 |
| Staff/Principal | < 60 | 60-90 | > 90 |
| Engineering Manager | < 50 | 50-75 | > 75 |
| Designer (all levels) | < 40 | 40-60 | > 60 |
| Product Manager | < 40 | 40-60 | > 60 |
| Executive/C-Level | < 90 | 90-120 | > 120 |

Note: For a team augmentation agency like Katapult, time-to-fill expectations may differ — client urgency often compresses timelines.

## Pipeline Conversion Benchmarks

| Stage Transition | Good | Average | Low |
|-----------------|------|---------|-----|
| Applied → Screen | 20-30% | 10-20% | < 10% |
| Screen → Interview | 40-60% | 25-40% | < 25% |
| Interview → Final Round | 30-50% | 20-30% | < 20% |
| Final Round → Offer | 40-60% | 25-40% | < 25% |
| Offer → Hired | 80-95% | 65-80% | < 65% |

## Source Effectiveness Benchmarks

| Source Type | Typical Volume | Typical Quality (pass screen) | Typical Hire Rate |
|------------|---------------|-------------------------------|-------------------|
| Employee Referrals | Low | 40-60% | 5-10% |
| Direct Sourcing | Medium | 30-50% | 3-7% |
| Job Boards | High | 10-25% | 1-3% |
| Agency/Recruiter | Low-Medium | 40-60% | 8-15% |
| Career Page | Medium | 15-30% | 2-5% |
| LinkedIn | High | 15-30% | 2-4% |
| Community/Events | Low | 25-40% | 3-6% |

## Interviewer Load Benchmarks

| Metric | Healthy | Overloaded |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Interviews per week | 2-5 | > 6 |
| Hours in interviews per week | 3-6 | > 8 |
| Unique candidates per week | 2-4 | > 5 |

## Offer Metrics Benchmarks

| Metric | Good | Average | Needs Attention |
|--------|------|---------|-----------------|
| Acceptance rate | > 85% | 70-85% | < 70% |
| Time to accept (days) | < 5 | 5-10 | > 10 |
| Offers extended per hire | 1.0-1.2 | 1.2-1.5 | > 1.5 |
